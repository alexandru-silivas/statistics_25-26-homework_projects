Statistics

Sapienza University of Rome

Alexandru Silivas | 2025/2026

This website presents a collection of assignments for the Statistics course at Sapienza University of Rome. The course explores essential statistical concepts applied to programming, mathematics, and cybersecurity. Through these projects, I aim to develop a deeper understanding of data analysis, probability, and their applications in modern digital systems.

This site serves as both a record of my progress and a resource for anyone interested in how statistical reasoning supports cybersecurity — from anomaly detection to predictive modeling.
